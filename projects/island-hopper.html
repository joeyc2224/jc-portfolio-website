Island Hopper VR

2023 | Immersive VR Speedboat Adventure

Overview

Island Hopper is an immersive Virtual Reality experience crafted during my time at the University of Adelaideâ€™s Realities Extended Unit. Built in Unity 3D, this project lets players embark on an exhilarating journey, speeding through an archipelago of tropical islands aboard a speedboat.

Players navigate a series of checkpoints as quickly as possible, floating on a custom-built water shader and physics system. Using Blender to create the required assets allowed me to experiment with a distinctive art style, resulting in a streamlined yet stunning collection of island landscapes optimised for VR performance. Enhanced by an immersive soundscape and dynamic lighting, Island Hopper transports players to the open ocean, giving them a sense of every wave and bump as they speed across the water.
